Taxi Driver

Seasons 1, 2 & 3  ·  Genre: Action, Crime, Vigilante

What It Is About

Kim Do-gi is a former special forces soldier whose mother was murdered — a crime technically punished, but never truly avenged. He now works as a driver for Rainbow Taxi Company, a secret vigilante organisation run by CEO Jang Sung-chul. Their service: if the law has failed you, they will make it right. Every case brings a new victim, a new injustice, and a new Do-gi undercover persona.

“Don’t die. Get revenge. We’re here to help.”

— Rainbow Taxi Company
