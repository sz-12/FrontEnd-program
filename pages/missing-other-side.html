Missing: The Other Side

Seasons 1 & 2  ·  Genre: Fantasy, Thriller, Mystery

What It Is About

Kim Wook is a smooth-talking con artist who stumbles into Duon Village — a hidden place invisible to the world, where spirits of missing people live in limbo. They cannot move on until their bodies are found and the truth behind their disappearance is uncovered. Wook can see them. He should not be able to. Nobody can explain why.

He teams up with detective Jang Pan-seok — a man who has spent years searching for his own missing daughter — and together they work case by case to solve each spirit’s death and give them what the world denied them: to finally be found.

“They were there. They existed. They deserved to be found.”

Themes

  • The injustice of being forgotten — in life and in death
  • Grief that refuses to close
  • Redemption through service to strangers
  • Family built by choice, not blood
